Work with me

What coaching actually looks like

Every session is yours. I follow your lead, work at your pace, and keep the focus on what matters most to you.

What is coaching?

Coaching isn't advice, and it isn't therapy. It's a structured conversation that helps you get clearer on what you want, understand what's getting in the way, and decide what to do next. I ask the questions — you find the answers.

Sessions are confidential and completely led by your agenda. There's no judgement, no pressure, and no prescribed outcome. Just space to think.

How a session works

1

Check in

We start by making sure you're settled and ready. A moment to arrive before we begin.

2

Set your goal for the session

You tell me what you'd like to focus on today. We agree on what a good outcome looks like.

3

Explore where you are now

We look honestly at your current situation — what's happening, how it feels, what you've already tried.

4

Open up your options

Together we explore possibilities you may not have considered. What could you do? What would help?

5

Decide on your next step

You leave with something concrete — a commitment to yourself, however small, that moves you forward.

6

Close and reflect

We end with a key takeaway from the session — something you'll carry with you.
