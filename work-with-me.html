Work with me

What coaching actually looks like

Every session is yours. I follow your lead, work at your pace, and keep the focus on what matters most to you.

What is coaching?

Coaching isn't advice, and it isn't therapy. It's a structured conversation that helps you get clearer on what you want, understand what's getting in the way, and decide what to do next. I ask the questions, you find the answers.

Sessions are confidential and completely led by your agenda. There's no judgement, no pressure, and no prescribed outcome. Just space to think.

How a session works

1

Settle in

Kettle on, shoulders down. A minute to land before we dive in.

2

Pick today's mission

You choose what we're tackling. We shake hands on what "job done" looks like.

3

Tell it like it is

No filters here. What's really going on, how it feels, what you've already tried.

4

Throw ideas at the wall

We get creative. What if you did this? What if you didn't? Nothing's off the table.

5

Pick your next move

You walk away with one clear step, big or small, that's actually yours.

6

Mic drop moment

We end on the one thing worth taking with you.
